Demon Slayer Mitarashi Dango

Description

These sweet and savory dango are a favorite snack among the Demon Slayers. They're perfect for a quick energy boost during training or a delicious treat after a long day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of glutinous rice flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix the glutinous rice flour, sugar, and water to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls and steam them for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce and sesame oil to create the glaze.
  4. Once the dango are cooked, brush them with the glaze.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve hot!
